Instructions:

Drag the 'Starting point' and 'Destination' markers to wherever you want - but both markers must be on the same route. Available routes are outlined in grey on the map.

Data (i.e. colour-coded traffic and animated vehicles) can be shown for only one route at a time.

The 'estimated trip time' for you to get from the starting point to the destination is shown farther down on the map page.

Colour codes for traffic:
Black - Less than 5 kmph. (5 kmph is a typical walking speed.)
Red - 5 kmph
Orange - 10 kmph
Yellow - 15 kmph
Green - 25 kmph and above
